Long-Lasting Tradition & True Coffee Love

The longing we feel for different, high-quality coffee and concepts led to the birth of the coffee in Coffee Shop brand. We are very excited about the combination of our thoughtfully designed concept with a historic mansion. Our goal is to offer the best coffee with the right concept to coffee enthusiasts. Coffee Shop was born in a vacant building from the 1800s in the Gaziemir district of Izmir, featuring peaceful workspaces, group gatherings by the fireplace, and a serene garden. Alongside our quality coffees, our croissants, made with special and exquisite Belgian chocolate, have become a brand in the region. Coffee in Coffee Shop is rapidly rising with its high-quality standard coffees, croissants, and special dessert varieties, driven by a unique passion and work discipline.
